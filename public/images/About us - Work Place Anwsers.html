Scott Taliaferro

Scott is the owner and founder of Workplace-Answers. He has spent 20+ years in the furniture industry as a dealer and as a manufacturer representative.

After 17 years with the MVK Group, Scott branched out on his own on June 1, 2017 and founded Workplace-Answers. He is responsible for all financial and accounting roles as well as developing and maintaining relationships with manufacturers and dealers.

He is very active with the dealer community and brings a broad product knowledge base and specification expertise to his dealers. He enjoys helping dealers find the perfect solution to their client’s needs.
